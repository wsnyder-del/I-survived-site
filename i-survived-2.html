Real Survival Science

Could You Survive?

Each book is packed with real facts. Here's what scientists say about survival:

🧠
Stay Calm
Panic burns energy and clouds judgment. Survivors focus on one small action at a time — the brain works best when it's not in overdrive.
💧
Find Water First
A human can survive 3 weeks without food but only 3 days without water. Locating a safe water source is always the top priority.
🔥
Signal for Help
Smoke during the day, fire at night. Three of anything is the universal distress signal rescuers are trained to spot.
🌊
Swim Parallel
Caught in a riptide? Never fight it head-on. Swim parallel to shore to escape the current, then angle back in.
⚡
Lightning Crouch
If caught outside, crouch low on the balls of your feet, ears covered, feet together. Never lie flat — ground current can travel through you.
🏔️
Stay Put
Lost hikers who stay near their last known position are found faster. Wandering makes search and rescue much harder.
